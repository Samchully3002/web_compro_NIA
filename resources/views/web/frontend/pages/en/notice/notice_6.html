Transfer Knowledge by PT NIA

DATE : 2023-08-25

PT Networks Indonesia Aku carried out a Knowledge Transfer which was held at the Indonesian Ministry of Transportation office on Friday, August 25th, 2023. It was attended by various parties from the Indonesian Ministry of Transportation, including the Center for Data and Information Technology (Pusdatin), Planning Section (Bagren), Directorate of Road Transportation Infrastructure.

Knowledge Transfer is carried out with the aim of providing knowledge to related parties regarding the systems that have been provided and developed by PT NIA. The systems that have been developed by PT NIA including Central Management, Terminal Management, Bus Company Management, Counter Management, Kiosk Management, Driver Application, Online Website and Bus Schedule System. In addition to Knowledge Transfer, PT NIA has conducted User Acceptance Testing (UAT) and field checks at the Kampung Rambutan Terminal in East Jakarta and the Leuwipanjang Terminal in West Java in collaboration with the Indonesian Ministry of Transportation.
