The Second Invitation Training, PT NIA and Indonesian Ministry of Transportation Visit South Korea

DATE : 2023-07-18

Following the success of the first invitation training in 2022, PT Networks Indonesia Aku (NIA), a subsidiary of Samchully Networks, held the second invitation training on July 12 - 18, 2023. The Indonesian Ministry of Transportation and representatives from the DKI Jakarta Transportation Department took part in this invitational training program.

PT NIA, in collaboration with the Ministry of Transportation and the DKI Jakarta Transportation Department, visited the Zentropy Company and the Hyundai Car Factory located in Jeonju and Ulsan as electric vehicle manufactures during this second invitational training. Apart from that, they have also visited the express bus transit stop in the rest area in Jeongan, Busan Central Bus Terminal, the transportation card company named T-money, and the Public Bus Parking Center in which provides bus garage and charging stations for buses. As we all know, Indonesia is now focusing on and targeting the Indonesian people to utilize electric vehicles. Of course, the provides experience and shares information regarding the use of electric vehicles and charging stations that can be applied in Indonesia.
